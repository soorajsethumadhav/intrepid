Royal Enfield Despatch (Limited edition)

An homage to the era of the Despatch Riders.
A limited-edition run of the Royal Enfield Classic.Riding long days and nights, motorcycle despatch riders delivered urgent messages and orders between headquarters and military units during the world wars. Their motorcycles included a classic Eneld - the Royal Eneld WD/CO. The limited edition Royal Eneld Despatch is an homage to the era they rode in. Only 200 motorcycles per colour have been produced - each bearing a unique embossed hallmark detailing particulars of its manufacture and a signature camouage pattern.
